Peri peri pizza

Description

Peri Peri Pizza is a spicy, flavorful pizza inspired by a traditional South African sauce made from African bird’s eye chili peppers, which were originally developed by Portuguese explorers in Southern Africa


Ingredients

  • Pizza Base
    • 1 (store-bought or homemade)
  • Peri Peri Sauce/Marinade
    • 2–4 tablespoons (can be store-bought or homemade)
  • Protein
    • 200–300g boneless chicken breast, cut into pieces or strips
    • Alternative options: paneer, mushrooms, or chorizo
  • Cheese
    • ½ cup mozzarella cheese (grated or sliced)
    • Optional: additional mozzarella for topping
    • Optional: Monterey Jack with jalapeño, Gouda
  • Vegetables
    • ½ red onion (sliced)
    • ½ red capsicum (sliced)
    • 1–2 cherry tomatoes (sliced)
    • ¼ cup sweet corn
    • ¼ cup jalapeños
    • ½ red bell pepper (diced)
    • 1–2 garlic cloves (minced)
    • 1–2 green chilies (sliced)
  • Sauce Base
    • 2–4 tablespoons pizza sauce, passata, or tomato ketchup
  • Seasonings
    • ½ teaspoon coarse salt
    • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1–2 teaspoons dried oregano
    • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (or apple cider vinegar)
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • ½ teaspoon dried chili flakes
    • ½ teaspoon sugar
    • ½ teaspoon dried mixed herbs
  • Oil
    • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil (for marinating or brushing)
  • Garnish
    • Fresh basil
    • Fresh coriander (cilantro)
    • Green onions (scallions)
    • A drizzle of peri peri aioli

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (180°C fan / gas mark 6 / 400°F).
  2. Prepare your protein:
    • Cut 200–300g of boneless chicken breast (or chosen alternative) into strips or pieces.
    • Marinate with 2–4 tablespoons peri peri sauce, 1 tablespoon lemon juice (or apple cider vinegar), 1–2 tablespoons olive oil, and 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste.
    • Let it sit for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat and cook the marinated protein until fully cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  4. Prepare the pizza base:
    • Spread 2–4 tablespoons of pizza sauce, passata, or tomato ketchup over the base.
    • Add a thin layer of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Add toppings:
    • Evenly distribute the cooked chicken (or protein alternative).
    • Top with sliced red onion, red capsicum, cherry tomatoes, sweet corn, jalapeños, red bell pepper, green chilies, and minced garlic.
  6. Season the pizza:
    • Sprinkle with coarse salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, dried oregano, chili flakes, dried mixed herbs, and a pinch of sugar (if desired).
  7. Add additional cheese if desired (e.g., more mozzarella, Gouda, or Monterey Jack with jalapeño).
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil, coriander (cilantro), green onions, or a drizzle of peri peri aioli.
  10. Slice and serve hot.

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10–15 minutes
Servings: 4 people
Temperature: 200°C (180°C fan / gas mark 6 / 400°F)
