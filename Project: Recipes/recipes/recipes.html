lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with wide, flat sheets of pasta layered with various fillings such as ragù (a meat sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, and cheeses like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, then baked until golden and bubbly


Ingredients

  • Meat
    • 1 to 2 pounds lean ground beef
    • 0.5 to 1 pound Italian sausage
    • Or a combination of both
  • Aromatics
    • ½ to 1 large onion, diced (about ¾ cup)
    • 2 to 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Tomatoes
    • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
    • 1 (14-ounce) can tomato sauce
    • 1 (28-ounce) can tomato paste
    • 2 (6.5-ounce) cans tomato sauce
    • 2 (14.5-ounce) cans whole tomatoes
  • Herbs & Seasonings
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano (or 2 teaspoons dried oregano)
    • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley, packed (preferably flat-leaf)
    • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    • ½ teaspoon dried basil
    • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
    • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
    • ½ to 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
    • ¼ teaspoon white sugar (or 1 teaspoon to ¼ cup sugar, to taste — optional)
  • Cheeses
    • 1 to 2 cups ricotta cheese
    • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
    • 2 to 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • ½ to 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Pasta
    • 1 (16-ounce) package lasagna noodles
    • 12 lasagna noodles (uncooked)
    • 1 (10-ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • Other
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • ½ cup chicken broth
    • ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Gather all ingredients and kitchen tools.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • In a large skillet or pot, cook ground beef and/or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned.
    • Add diced onion and garlic; sauté until softened and fragrant.
    • Stir in all tomato products (crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, whole tomatoes) and mix well.
    • Add herbs and seasonings: oregano, parsley, Italian seasoning, basil, fennel seeds, black pepper, salt, and sugar (if using).
    • Simmer the sauce on low for 30 to 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions (if not using no-boil noodles). Drain and set aside.
  5. Prepare the cheese mixture:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, cottage cheese, ¼ cup Parmesan cheese, and 1 beaten egg. Mix well.
  6. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Layer noodles, cheese mixture, mozzarella, and sauce. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
    • Sprinkle remaining Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover the dish with foil and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 30 to 40 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10 to 20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 to 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Extras

Prep Time: 25 to 30 minutes
Cook Time: 1 hour 10 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes
Servings: 8 to 10
